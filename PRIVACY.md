# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

## Overview

Breathing Exercise Guide is a simple breathing exercise app designed to help users relax, focus, and sleep. This app stores limited information on your device to support breathing sessions, preferences, and progress tracking.

## Information We Collect

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

- Completed breathing session history
- Session duration and completion timestamps
- App preferences such as sound, haptics, and voice guidance

The current version of the app does not require account creation.

## How Information Is Used

Stored information is used only to:

- Track completed breathing sessions
- Show progress such as streaks, total minutes, and session counts
- Remember your settings and preferences
- Improve your in-app experience

## Data Sharing

Breathing Exercise Guide does not sell your personal information.

The current version of the app does not send breathing session history to third-party advertising or analytics services.

## Data Storage

Your session history and app settings are stored locally on your device unless future versions introduce cloud sync or platform integrations.

## Your Choices

You can remove the app from your device to delete locally stored app data.

If the app adds Apple Health, cloud sync, or other external integrations in a future release, this privacy policy should be updated before release.

## Children's Privacy

The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.
