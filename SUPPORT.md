## App Summary

Breathing Exercise Guide helps users complete short guided breathing exercises such as Calm Breathing, Box Breathing, 4-7-8 Relax, Stress Reset, and Sleep Wind Down.

## Common Help Topics

### Voice Guidance

- Voice Guidance must be enabled in Settings.
- Your device must have audible output enabled.
- If your phone is muted or audio output is redirected, spoken cues may not be heard.

### Haptics

- Haptics work only on supported iPhone hardware.
- Haptics must be enabled in the app settings.

### Progress Tracking

- Progress updates after completed sessions.
- Ending a session early may not count as a completed session.

### App Icon Not Updating

- If a newly installed build still shows an old icon, remove the app from the device and install it again.

## Troubleshooting Steps

If the app is not behaving as expected:

1. Close and reopen the app.
2. Run a short breathing session again.
3. Confirm the relevant setting is enabled.
4. Reinstall the app if the issue continues during development builds.
